The Dead Pool Draft

  1. All teams must participate in Draft day, whether in person or over electronic means
  2. Draft is to be done in the following order 1, 2, 3, 4, 4, 3, 2, 1
  3. The winner of the previous year will choose in the number 4 position
  4. Chosen persons must meet the following requirements:
  5. Scoring: When one of your choices dies you take 100 and subtract thier age that is their "score,"
  6. All scores are final at the stroke of Midnight on Dec. 31st and the team with the highest score wins.
  7. The "Sally Farmiloe Rule," all picks must be defended by the picker and the rest of the draft party will be able to challenge the pickers knowledge of their choice.
  8. The "Mellisa" rule, all deaths are to be self reported, if you don't know your pick died it does not score.
